Introduction:

Nepal is a himalayan country in South Asia situated between India and China. Kathmandu is the capital of Nepal. It has an estimated population of 30 million. Nepal is divided into three regions - himalayan, hilly and terai. Mount Everest, the highest peak in the world is in Nepal.

Kathmandu is the capital and lagest city of Nepal. Nepali is the nation's official language. Buddha was born in Lumbini, Nepal.

The new constitution of Nepal was adopted in 2015 and it has established Nepal as a federal secular parliamentary republic divided into seven provinces. Prior to that, Nepal was a hindu kingdom ruld by the Shah dynasty.
